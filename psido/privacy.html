Privacy Policy

How we handle, process, and secure your data.

Last Updated: May 2026

Psido Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("Psido", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Psido.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use our website or services. This includes:

  • Contact Information (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
  • Professional Information (e.g., job title, company name)
  • Technical Data (e.g., IP address, browser type) automatically collected when you visit our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our cybersecurity services
  • Respond to your requests, comments, or inquiries
  • Send administrative communications regarding your services
  • Comply with legal obligations under Indian law

3. Data Security

As a cybersecurity company, data security is our highest priority. We implement enterprise-grade security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest, zero-trust network access, and continuous monitoring to protect your personal information against unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners.
